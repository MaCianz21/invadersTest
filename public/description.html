Create a new room by clicking on the 'create room' button or join a room by clicking on the 'join room' button.
Once joined the room, wait for all players to connect.
When the room is full you have one minute to kill as many aliens as possible.
                  COMMANDS
SHOOT      [LEFT MOUSE BUTTON][ARROW UP]
RECHARGE AMMO     [R][ARROW DOWN]
MOVEMENT [ARROW LEFT][ARROW RIGHT][MOUSE]
